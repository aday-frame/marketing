07 Why Frame exists

The new operating standardfor private wealth.

Private assets — estates, vessels, aircraft — have run on paper, text messages, binders, and tribal knowledge for a hundred years. Every property is different. Every vessel is different. There has never been a standard.

When the estate manager leaves, the knowledge leaves with them. When the captain rotates, the rhythm restarts. Operational excellence is rebuilt every season, from memory.

Frame is the new operating standard. One operating layer for every asset, every vendor, every procedure. Repeatable across the portfolio, durable across staff turnover. The way private operations should work.
